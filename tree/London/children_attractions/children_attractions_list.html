Attractions for kids

  • The Diana Memorial Playground

    This playground is inspired by Peter Pan and is designed for kids up to the age of 12. It features a pirate ship, teepees, and a sensory trail.

    Visit website

  • SEA LIFE London Aquarium

    SEA LIFE London Aquarium has over 600 species of marine life, including sharks, turtles, and penguins. It also features interactive exhibits and a glass walkway over the shark tank.

    Visit website

  • London Zoo

    London Zoo is home to over 750 species of animals, including gorillas, tigers, and penguins. It also features interactive exhibits, a children's zoo, and a playground.

    Visit website
