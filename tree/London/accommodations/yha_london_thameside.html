YHA London Thameside

Stay at YHA London Thameside for a budget-friendly accommodation option with stunning views of the River Thames. Located just a short walk from the trendy neighbourhood of Bermondsey, this hostel offers comfortable dormitory rooms and private rooms at affordable prices. Enjoy free Wi-Fi, a self-catering kitchen, and a peaceful garden on site. Perfect for budget-conscious travellers looking for a peaceful location with great views.
