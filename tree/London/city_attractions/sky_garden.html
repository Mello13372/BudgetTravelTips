Sky Garden

Are you a budget traveller looking for a unique and breathtaking experience in London? Look no further than Sky Garden, the city's highest public garden and one of its most popular attractions. Located atop the iconic Walkie Talkie building in the heart of the financial district, Sky Garden offers stunning panoramic views of the city skyline, lush greenery, and a variety of dining and entertainment options.\nSky Garden opened in 2015 and quickly became a must-visit destination for locals and tourists alike. The garden spans three floors and features over 2,000 plants from around the world, including exotic species like the Chilean wine palm and the Australian tree fern. The space is designed to be a peaceful oasis in the midst of the bustling city, with plenty of seating areas and natural light.\nBut the real draw of Sky Garden is the views. From the top of the building, visitors can see some of London's most famous landmarks, including the Tower Bridge, the Shard, and St. Paul's Cathedral. The view is especially stunning at sunset, when the sky turns shades of pink and orange and the city lights up below.\nIn addition to the garden itself, Sky Garden offers a variety of dining options, from casual cafes to upscale restaurants. Visitors can enjoy a drink or a meal while taking in the views, or attend one of the many events hosted in the space, such as live music performances or yoga classes.\nVisiting Sky Garden is a truly unique and unforgettable experience. Whether you're a nature lover, a foodie, or just looking for a new perspective on the city, Sky Garden has something for everyone. So why not add it to your London itinerary and see the city from a whole new angle?
